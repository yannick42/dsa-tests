2-SAT problem
a tool to display the implication graph of a 2-SAT formula

Here, select multiple pairs of elements while choosing their relationship. If the satisfiability is not possible, a message will appear, it means there no way to gather at least 2 person according to their liking. (you can use Ctrl+Click to select an other person, and also doubleclick a name to remove it).

- If a pair are compatible, we have \(a \lor b\) which can be rewritten as \(\lnot a \rightarrow b\) and \(\lnot b \rightarrow a\).
- Otherwise \(a \lor \lnot b\) becomes \(\lnot a \rightarrow \lnot b\) and \( b \rightarrow a\)

If a literal (a, b, ...) and its negation are in the same strongly connected component, then no solution is possible.
