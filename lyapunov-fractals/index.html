Page based on Desdenova's Youtube video (Aug. 16th 2022) contribution to SoME2
  • Invented in 1989 by Mario Markus, and appeared in the Scientific American (popular science) as a recreational math. topic in 1991.
  • Derived from the logistic map (an iterated function) : \[ x_{n+1}=r x_n (1 - x_n) \]
  • Where \(r\) values vary between A, B and C following a selected schema
  • The lyapunov characteristic exponent (1892) is calculated to fill each colored pixels, it's a rate of separation of infinitesimally close trajectories (some colors represent stability, superstability or chaos)
    \[ \lambda(x_0)=\lim\limits_{n\rightarrow \infty}\dfrac{1}{n}\sum_{i=0}^{n-1}\ln |f'(x_i)| \]
